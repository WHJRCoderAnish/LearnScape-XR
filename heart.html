❤️ The Heart System

Your heart is an amazing organ! It pumps blood throughout your entire body about 100,000 times per day. With every heartbeat, oxygen-rich blood flows to your organs and tissues, delivering life-giving oxygen and nutrients. When your heart contracts (beats), it forces blood out to your body. When it relaxes, blood returns to fill it again. This continuous pumping action is what keeps you alive!
